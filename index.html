Hello.

Hi, I'm Mayowa. I like to design all kinds of stuff.

I was born in 1989, the same year as the internet, but that's probably just a coincidence! I was born and raised in Nigeria, I live in the U.S. and study Arts & Media Informatics and Business & Management in Scotland.

As a kid I came this :: close to being a musician! But alas it was not meant to be. So now I spend my days listening to every type of music on the planet, and forever trying to unite my art and my promising music career... albeit not being musically inclined whatsoever.

I went from traditional drawing and painting, and eased into graphic design, motion design, and web design. Of late I've gotten myself into typography, photography, and videography. Which one do I prefer? That's quite the conundrum. I'm passionate about what I do and always eager to push my limits. I enjoy collaborating with people.

Oh, and I quote lyrics. A lot.
